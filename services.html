Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in my free estimate? +

Our completely free estimate includes a visit from our certified technician to audit your AC system, identify any issues or requirements, and provide you with a transparent quote outlining parts and labor needed with no hidden costs.

How often should my systems be serviced? +

We recommend servicing your air conditioning system at least once a year. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance, extends the lifespan of the equipment, and helps maintain high energy efficiency, particularly before the peak summer season.

How do I reduce my cooling costs? +

Regular wet servicing, ensuring clean indoor and outdoor unit filters, checking refrigerant levels, and using programmable settings effectively can significantly lower your cooling costs over the season.
